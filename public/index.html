Trading Plan

My trading plan is a set of rules that tells me what type of market to trade, what conditions need to be present, when to enter and exit a trade, how to manage risk, and what trades are not worth taking.

1 Premarket Routine

2 Chart Process

  • Mark the higher timeframe trend (BOS, MSS)
  • Identify the swing range of the higher timeframe
  • Ask: are we trading in premium, discount, or midrange?
  • Mark liquidity (previous day high/low, equal highs/lows)
  • Read the story the market is telling — build a narrative
  • Determine if we're in continuation or reversal phase
  • Define your zones (supply/demand, liquidity, flip zones)
  • Determine your entry and exit positions
  • Define your invalidation
  • Risk only 1% of your account

3 Entry Criteria

  • Wait for price to mitigate the HTF/MTF POI then drop to the LTF
  • Look for liquidity during the reversal phase and on the left side of the market (previous price action)
  • Ideally see the market take out liquidity during the reversal/pullback, situated on the left side
  • Look for a liquidity sweep and market structure shift
  • Identify your zone (demand/supply, flip zone, liquidity zone)
  • Place your buy/sell limit order at the zone, or wait for mitigation + reversal candle

4 Exit Criteria

  • Stop loss: below/above the entry candle, at the swept high/low, at the protected high/low, or below demand / above supply
  • Take profit: at the opposing supply/demand zone, or at the next high/low

5 Post Market Routine

6 Plan Questions

  • What market conditions do I want to trade?
  • What makes a setup valid?
  • How do I manage the trade?
  • Where do I take profit?
  • When should I stay out completely?
