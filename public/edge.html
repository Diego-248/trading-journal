What Is an Edge?

Far from being a magical, holy-grail strategy that never loses, an edge is simply a statistical advantage built on two primary metrics — Win Rate and Risk-to-Reward (R:R) Ratio — which interact to create a positive expectancy and profitable trading strategy on a long run over a large sample of trades.
